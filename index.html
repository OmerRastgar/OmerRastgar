About me

What drives me is the sheer wonder of building something beyond our imagination. For instance, in Formula One, there is no intrinsic value in building a vehicle that pushes the limits of technology. The reason it is fascinating is that we are testing the limits of our own creativity, trying to push the bounds of what is merely possible. I get this very feeling from the field of cybersecurity as well. There is no one solution that fits all when viewing different projects that have been presented. The complexity keeps me hooked, and the macgyver-like nature keeps me intrigued to keep coming back for more unbelievable solutions. The sense of community is overwhelming as everyone tries to help improve privacy and security. My journey within in different discipline of cyber security has given me greater appreciation of team work and collaboration. As a self-taught learner I have had the opportunity to learn from amazing people and I want to pay the learning experience forward by providing value to the community.
